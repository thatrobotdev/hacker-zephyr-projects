iOS Instructions

  1. Open the "Contacts" app.
  2. Open the contact you want to upload.
  3. Scroll to the bottom, and click "Share contact".
  4. Using AirDrop or another method, share the file with your computer.
  5. Click the "Browse" button below, and select the .vcf file.
  6. Click "Submit".

    7. Happy hacking!
